Toast-Notification-With-Progress-Bar

By: Admin mars 10, 2020

A Toast Notification with a Progress Bar is a small, unobtrusive message that appears temporarily on the screen to provide feedback or updates to the user. It typically includes a brief message and an optional progress bar that visually indicates the duration before the notification disappears. The notification is styled to be prominent yet non-disruptive, often positioned at the top or bottom of the screen. The progress bar smoothly decreases in width as time passes, giving users a clear sense of how long the message will remain visible. These notifications are often used for confirming actions, displaying success or error messages, and providing real-time updates without requiring user intervention.
